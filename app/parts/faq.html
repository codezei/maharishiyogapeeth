Frequently Asked Questions

How long should you do yoga before starting yoga teacher training ?

Ideally, you should have already attended a few yoga classes before starting your yoga teacher training. We encourage you to focus on completing your training. Western yoga is very different from traditional yoga. Our classes build slowly so you can learn all about asanas, pranayama, meditation, and more, from beginner to advanced.
