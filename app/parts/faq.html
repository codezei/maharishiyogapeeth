Frequently Asked Questions

How long should you do yoga before starting yoga teacher training ?

Ideally, you should have already attended a few yoga classes before starting your yoga teacher training. We encourage you to focus on completing your training. Western yoga is very different from traditional yoga. Our classes build slowly so you can learn all about asanas, pranayama, meditation, and more, from beginner to advanced.

Which yoga style do you teach?

Maharishi Yoga Peeth teaches traditional yoga, also known as hathasana, and the philosophy of Ashtanga Yoga (the eight parts). Our approach is to look at the individual and teach you how to bring perfect health to your body and others. First, we need to understand the common complaints and problems of today. Once you understand this and understand the benefits and caveats of asana and pranayama techniques, you can decide which techniques to use to create healthy, physical, and mental well-being.

Which courses do you have for yoga teachers?

A good yoga teacher remains a student for life, it is important to keep growing, learning, and developing. If you are already a certified yoga teacher, you may want to improve your teaching skills and deepen your understanding of Therapy Her Yoga, Pranayama, and Ayurveda consultations.

What can I expect from a Yoga Teacher Training at Maharishi Yoga Peeth?

A truly transformative journey to your heart can be expected. By deepening your knowledge and practice of traditional yoga, emotional block, cures, and Ayurveda, you will learn how to create mental peace, tranquility, wellness, and joy in your daily life. Our instructors and team are here to provide one-on-one guidance to help you reconnect, de-stress and unplug. Unique and open-minded in India, Get ready for a life-changing journey !
