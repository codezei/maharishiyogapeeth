Ashram

We are Located in the Centre of Rishikesh, which is the Yoga Capital of The World. Maharishi Yoga Peeth is a Place to Relax, Rejuvenate and Re-Balance. It is an Amalgamation of Serene Spiritual Yoga Ashram Environment with a Professional Training Institutional Approach. We Strive to Keep the Energy of the Ashram Pure and Untainted so that the Training Practice Brings about Spiritual Transformation. Therefore, Try to Come with an Open Mind, Soft Heart and a Strong Spine.

Yoga Shala

Our Spacious Well Ventilated Yoga Shala Has a 360° Forest Moutain View, with Ample Natural Light and Fresh Air. It is Fully Equipped with all Necessary Yoga Accessories and Props, Smart Classroom Facilities for Lectures and a Small Library for the Bookworms.

Accommodation

  • Our Boarding Facility is Completely Safe for Everyone. The Rooms are Clean and Spacious with Ensuite Washrooms and 24/7 Hot Water Facility.
  • All Rooms have a Beautiful Mountain Or Ganga View.
  • We can Accommodate you a Day Prior to The Start of The Program. If You are Arriving Earlier, You will Have to Manage Your Own Accommodation, Else we will Provide Stay at an Additional Cost.

Food

  • At Maharishi Yoga Peeth, We Strictly Follow Vegetarian Diet. Food will be Provided Thrice a Day (Breakfast, Lunch and Dinner) with special Herbal Tea. The Food Provided at the Ashram is Delicious – Ayurvedic Meals. However, We do Try our Best to Accommodate Special Food Requirements Such as Lactose Intolerant or Gluten Intolerant.
  • The Food We Consume is Very Significant in The Yoga Practice. Therefore, We Make a Conscious Effort in Acquiring Organically Grown Fruits and Vegetables. It is also Important How the Food is Prepared. The Mind of Chef in the Preparation of the Food has an Impact on The Quality of Food Cooked and Served. Even the Process of Food Consumption has an Immense Impact on the Yoga Practice.
  • Considering all the Aspects, We have Been Making Conscious Efforts in Offering Food as a Sacred Process of Enhancing Yoga Practice
